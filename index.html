Founded in March 2018, AFA Academy Sdn Bhd (Registration: 1272945P) has been at the forefront of safety training in Malaysia. Based in Seri Kembangan, Selangor, our dedicated team of 2-10 professionals is committed to delivering high-quality, innovative training solutions.

We specialize in first aid, CPR, AED training, fire safety, emergency response, and various workplace safety programs. Our unique gamified and digitalized approach sets us apart as Malaysia's pioneer in interactive safety education.
